Overview

FoodRun is CSUF's leading online food rating website created for professor Avery's front-end engineering class. FoodRun allows food critics all over the world discover recipes, food inspiration, and rate pictures you love! In addition to reviews, you may also use FoodRun in order to upload your own pictures and receive feedback and comments.
